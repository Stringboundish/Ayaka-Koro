Ayaka Koro

Stats

  • Class: Brawler (Level 4)
  • Race: Ursine
  • HP: 60
  • AC: 18
Arms and Armor
  • Dagger Damage1d4; Critical 19-20/x2; Type piercing or slashing; Range Increment 10 ft. (thrown); You get a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand skill checks made to conceal a dagger on your body.
  • Lamellar Light Armor - Armor Bonus +4
  • Sling Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium) Critical x2 Type bludgeoning. Range Increment 50 ft. (projectile). You can hurl ordinary stones with a sling, but stones are not as dense or as round as bullets. Thus, such an attack deals damage as if the weapon were designed for a creature one size category smaller than you and you take a –1 penalty on attack rolls.
  • Healing Potion (Minor) x2 The potion restores 1d8 Hit Points.
Traits and Feats
  • Dirty Fighter Trait: When you hit a foe you are flanking, you deal 1 additional point of damage (this damage is added to your base damage, and is multiplied on a critical hit). This additional damage is a trait bonus.
  • Suprise Weapon Trait: You gain a +2 trait bonus on attack rolls with improvised weapons.
  • Improved Unarmed Strike Combat: You are considered to be armed even when unarmed—you do not provoke attacks of opportunity when you attack foes while unarmed. Your unarmed strikes can deal lethal or nonlethal damage, at your choice.
  • Bear's Endurance When you regain hit points, you recover additional hit points equal to half your Constitution modifier (minimum +1).
  • Improved Grapple Combat: You do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a grapple combat maneuver. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on checks made to grapple a foe. You also receive a +2 bonus to your Combat Maneuver Defense whenever an opponent tries to grapple you.
